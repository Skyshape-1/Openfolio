## Introduction: IQC PLM

Welcome to the Intellectual Quant Challenge (IQC) in Probability, Logic, and Mathematics (PLM). This blog post series is dedicated to exploring a collection of challenging problems that test your analytical skills and logical reasoning. Whether you're a student, professional, or enthusiast, these problems are designed to stimulate your mind and enhance your problem-solving abilities.

This page is created by 3 math/logic-loving friends who decided to share their passion for problem-solving with the world. 
We believe that engaging with challenging problems is a great way to learn and grow intellectually.
