Welcome to my personal photo gallery documenting an unforgettable winter trip from Wuhan to Beijing and Harbin. This collection captures the essence of the journey, showcasing iconic landmarks, cultural experiences, and the breathtaking beauty of the Harbin Ice and Snow Festival.
